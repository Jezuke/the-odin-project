Mochi Images

This page covers common image formats.

JPGs

JPG images are good for displaying photos without increasing file size by sacrificing some detail through compression. However, they don't allow for transparent pixels.

GIFs

GIFs are the go-to for animations, but are limited in color palette thus preventing a high-level of detail.

PNGs

PNGs are great for anything that's not a photo or animated such as a logo or diagram as they include a higher-level of quality and allow opacity.

SVGs

SVGs are vector-based images compared to pixel-based images above so that means they can scale up and down in any dimension without losing quality and should be used in place of PNGs as often as possible.
